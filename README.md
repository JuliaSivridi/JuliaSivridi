## About

Software developer by training — M.Tech. in Software Engineering, with early career years building internal tools and production software in industrial environments. Spent the middle chapter in systems analysis and requirements engineering: 7+ years translating complex business domains into clear technical specifications across IT and system integration companies.

Now back at the keyboard full-time. Building PWA, Android apps, and Telegram bots — and using AI-assisted development as a deliberate multiplier, not a shortcut.

Currently studying Business Information Technology at South-Eastern Finland University of Applied Sciences.
